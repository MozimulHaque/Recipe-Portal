Banana Pancakes

Banana pancakes are a delicious breakfast dish made by incorporating mashed ripe bananas into a traditional pancake batter. The bananas add a natural sweetness and moistness to the pancakes, enhancing their flavor and texture. Typically, the batter consists of flour, eggs, milk, baking powder, and sometimes a hint of vanilla extract or cinnamon for extra taste. Once cooked on a griddle or frying pan, these pancakes become fluffy, slightly sweet, and wonderfully aromatic. They're often served with toppings like fresh sliced bananas, maple syrup, honey, whipped cream, or nuts to complement their flavor profile. It's a popular choice for breakfast or brunch, loved for its simplicity and delightful taste.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Dry and Wet Ingredients

In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. In another bowl, mash the ripe banana thoroughly. Add the milk, egg, melted butter or oil, and vanilla extract or cinnamon if using. Mix well until combined.

Step 2: Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients

Pour the wet ingredients mixture into the bowl with the dry ingredients. Gently fold and mix the ingredients until just combined. It's okay if the batter has a few lumps; overmixing can make the pancakes tough.

Step 3: Cook the Pancakes

Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease the surface with butter or cooking spray. Once the pan is hot, pour about 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake onto the skillet. You can adjust the amount based on your desired pancake size. Cook the pancakes for about 2-3 minutes on the first side. When bubbles start to form on the surface and the edges look set, flip the pancakes gently. Cook for another 1-2 minutes on the second side until golden brown and cooked through. Transfer the cooked pancakes to a plate and continue with the remaining batter. Serve the banana pancakes warm with toppings like sliced bananas, maple syrup, honey, nuts, or whipped cream.

ingredients

1 cup all-purpose flour

1 tablespoon sugar

2 teaspoons baking powder

1/4 teaspoon salt

1 ripe banana, mashed

1 cup milk

1 large egg

2 tablespoons melted butter or oil

Optional: 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract or a pinch of cinnamon
