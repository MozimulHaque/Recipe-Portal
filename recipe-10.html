Fritters

Fritters are delightful savory or sweet treats made by frying various ingredients in batter. The batter typically consists of flour, eggs, milk or water, and sometimes baking powder or spices. Ingredients such as vegetables, fruits, meat, or seafood are commonly used to make fritters. These ingredients are dipped or mixed into the batter and then deep-fried until they achieve a crispy, golden-brown exterior. Savory fritters often include items like corn, zucchini, or cheese, while sweet fritters can feature apples, bananas, or berries. Fritters are versatile and enjoyed as snacks, appetizers, or even desserts in many cuisines around the world. Their crispy texture and diverse flavors make them a popular choice for a quick and tasty treat.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Batter

In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, cornmeal, baking powder, salt, and pepper. Add the corn kernels, grated cheese (if using), and chopped scallions or onions into the dry ingredients and mix well. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the egg and milk. Pour the egg and milk mixture into the dry ingredients and corn mixture. Stir until just combined. The batter should be thick and well incorporated.

Step 2: Fry the Fritters

Heat oil in a frying pan or skillet over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, spoon a portion of the batter into the pan to form individual fritters, using about 2-3 tablespoons of batter for each fritter. Flatten the batter slightly with the back of the spoon to form a round shape. Fry the fritters for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they turn golden brown and crispy. Remove the fritters from the pan and place them on a plate lined with paper towels to drain any excess oil.

Step 3: Serve and Enjoy

Once all fritters are cooked, serve them warm. You can optionally garnish with extra chopped scallions or a dollop of sour cream, salsa, or your favorite dipping sauce. Enjoy the crispy and flavorful corn fritters as a tasty snack or as a side dish with your favorite meal.

ingredients

1 cup corn kernels (fresh, frozen, or canned and drained)

1/2 cup all-purpose flour

1/4 cup cornmeal

1/4 cup grated cheese (optional)

1/4 cup chopped scallions or onions

1 egg

Salt and pepper to taste

Oil for frying
