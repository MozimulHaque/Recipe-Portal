Margherita Pizza

The Margherita pizza is a quintessential Italian dish renowned for its simplicity and vibrant flavors. It features a thin crust adorned with minimal yet high-quality ingredients: a base of San Marzano tomato sauce, slices of fresh mozzarella cheese, and whole basil leaves. The combination of these components reflects the colors of the Italian flag—red, white, and green—symbolizing the unity of flavors. This classic pizza celebrates the harmony of tangy tomatoes, creamy cheese, and fragrant basil, resulting in a delightful culinary experience that embodies the essence of Italian cuisine.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Pizza Dough

In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-1.5 hours, or until it doubles in size.

Step 2: Assemble the Pizza

Preheat your oven to the highest temperature it can go (usually around 500°F or higher). Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness and shape. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust. Arrange the sliced fresh mozzarella on top of the sauce. Tear the fresh basil leaves and scatter them over the cheese. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper.

Step 3: Bake and Serve

Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for about 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned. Once done, remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a minute or two. Drizzle some extra-virgin olive oil over the pizza. Slice and serve your Margherita pizza hot, savoring the delightful combination of flavors!

ingredients

2 ¼ teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast

1 ½ cups warm water

4 cups all-purpose flour

1 teaspoon salt

2 tablespoons olive oil

1 ½ cups tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes

8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced

Fresh basil leaves

Salt and pepper to taste

Extra-virgin olive oil for drizzling
