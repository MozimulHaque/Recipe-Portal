Banana Pudding

Banana pudding is a classic Southern dessert that features layers of vanilla pudding, sliced ripe bananas, and either vanilla wafers or ladyfingers. The pudding is typically made with a custard base, combining milk, sugar, and cornstarch or flour, then flavored with vanilla extract. Layering the pudding with slices of bananas and cookies creates a comforting and indulgent treat. The dessert is often served chilled, allowing the flavors to meld together and the cookies to soften slightly, resulting in a creamy, fruity, and satisfying dessert experience.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Pudding Base

In a medium saucepan, whisk together the sugar, flour, and salt. In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg yolks and milk until well combined. Gradually pour the egg and milk mixture into the saucepan with the dry ingredients, whisking constantly to prevent lumps. Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens enough to coat the back of a spoon (about 8-10 minutes). Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in the vanilla extract. Let the pudding base cool slightly.

Step 2: Assemble the Banana Pudding

In a serving dish or a trifle bowl, create a layer of vanilla wafers or ladyfingers at the bottom. Add a layer of sliced bananas over the cookies. Pour a portion of the pudding over the bananas and cookies, covering them evenly. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of pudding on top.

Step 3: Chill and Serve

Cover the banana pudding with plastic wrap, ensuring the wrap touches the surface of the pudding to prevent a skin from forming. Refrigerate the banana pudding for at least 2-3 hours, allowing it to set and chill thoroughly. Before serving, garnish the top with additional banana slices or crushed cookies if desired.

ingredients

3-4 ripe bananas, sliced

1 box (about 12 ounces) vanilla wafers or ladyfingers

2/3 cup granulated sugar

1/4 cup all-purpose flour

1/4 teaspoon salt

2 cups whole milk

4 large egg yolks

1 teaspoon vanilla extract
